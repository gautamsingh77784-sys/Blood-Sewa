Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last updated: April 29, 2026
🛡️

Your privacy matters to us

Blood Sewa is built to save lives while respecting your privacy. We collect only what is necessary to connect donors with recipients, and you remain in full control of your data at all times.

1. Introduction

Blood Sewa ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Blood Sewa mobile application.

By using Blood Sewa, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register and use Blood Sewa:

  • Full name and phone number
  • Email address (optional)
  • Blood group and location (city, state, country)
  • Age, weight, and last donation date
  • Profile availability status
  • Organization memberships and activity
  • FCM token for push notifications
  • Google account ID (if you sign in with Google)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Connect blood donors with recipients in need
  • Send emergency blood request notifications to nearby donors
  • Display your donor profile to people searching for blood
  • Manage your organization memberships and announcements
  • Send push notifications for blood requests and alerts
  • Improve app performance and user experience

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Your name, blood group, and location are visible to other app users when you are marked as "Available to Donate"
  • Your phone number is visible to others unless you enable "Hide Phone Number"
  • Emergency SOS alerts include your name, phone, blood group, and location to notify nearby donors
  • Organization members can see your profile within the organization

5. Your Privacy Controls

You have full control over your privacy settings within the app:

🔒 Hide Phone Number
Shows •••••••••• instead of your number to other users
📍 Hide City
Shows only state and country, not your city
💬 Hide WhatsApp
Hides your WhatsApp number from your profile
🔴 Availability Toggle
Set yourself as unavailable to stop appearing in donor searches
🗑️ Delete Account
Permanently delete your account and all associated data

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted)
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain text passwords
  • JWT tokens are used for secure authentication
  • Chat messages are not stored on our servers (relay-only system — deleted after delivery)
  • SOS history is automatically deleted after 2 days

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All your personal information is permanently deleted
  • Your blood request posts are removed
  • Your donation history is erased
  • You are removed from all organizations
  • SOS history older than 2 days is automatically deleted regardless

8. Push Notifications

Blood Sewa uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications. These include:

  • Emergency blood request alerts from nearby users
  • Organization announcements from groups you have joined
  • Blood post notifications for your area

You can disable notifications from your device settings at any time.

9. Third-Party Services

Blood Sewa uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — push notifications and authentication
  • Google Sign-In — optional login method
  • OpenStreetMap — map display in blood request details

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

10. Children's Privacy

Blood Sewa is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (view your profile in the app)
  • Update or correct your information (edit profile)
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Opt out of notifications (device settings)
  • Control your privacy settings within the app

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. Continued use of Blood Sewa after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
